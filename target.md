# FIT5122 Assignment 2 — Target & Working Record

## Case Study
**2021 Facebook Global Outage** (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger)
- ~6-hour global outage on 4–5 October 2021
- Cause: configuration change to backbone routers → BGP withdrawal → DNS unreachable
- Cascading effects: internal Workplace comms, badge/security access, employee tooling all down
- Manual on-site reset at California data centre required to recover
- Share price dropped 4.9%; Zuckerberg lost ~$6bn personal wealth

- **Delivery:** Live 10-minute group presentation, all members speak, no scripts

- **Collaboration evidence:** Miro board (or equivalent)
- **Due:** Tuesday Week 7, 11:55pm on Moodle; presented in class Week 7

## Required Analytical Coverage
1. **System Overview** — Facebook backbone routing as point of systemic failure aggregation; stakeholder impact map
2. **Failure Analysis (RCA)** — direct + escalated downstream business impacts
3. **Process Breakdown** — QA, deployment methodology, change management controls, Business Continuity Planning
4. **Critical Factor Evaluation** — interdependence of reliability events, cascading failures, accountability boundaries (Facebook vs. infrastructure vs. dependent third parties)
5. **Strategic Recommendations** — transparent, structured, responsive risk framework; explicit trade-off discussion (rigour vs. Time-to-Market)
